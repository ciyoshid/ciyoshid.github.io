Introduction

Tipsy

Tipsy is a magazine that features various classic cocktail recipes, as well as a guide to currently trending and popular cocktails. The magazine also features tips and tricks to master the art of bartending. In addition to recipes and tips, Tipsy will also examine the history behind various cocktails, including details about both the people and ingredidents regarding the story of each cocktail's creation.
